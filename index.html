What makes our products awesome?

No Gluten

Saving the day means no time for belly aches, so no super power-sapping wheat or gluten in our snacks.

No Refined Sugar

There’s no time for afternoon-napping when you’re out saving the world. For all the energy, without the slump, we only use whole fruits (and occasionally raw honey) to sweeten our products.

No Dairy

Cows don’t usually make great sidekicks (they mooove to slowly), so we decided to leave them out of this adventure.

Raw (mostly!)

Super powers require super fuel – the kind that raw, enzyme-rich food provides. We aim to keep our products as raw as possible, so you can keep on fighting when everyone else calls it a day.

Vegan (mostly!)

We want our heroes to maximise their plant-based powers. Occasionally, however, we call on our black and yellow buzzing friends to help with an extra immunity and natural sweetness boost.
