What makes our products awesome?

No Gluten

Saving the day means no time for belly aches, so no super power-sapping wheat or gluten in our snacks.

No Refined Sugar

There’s no time for afternoon-napping when you’re out saving the world. For all the energy, without the slump, we only use whole fruits (and occasionally raw honey) to sweeten our products.

No Dairy

Cows don’t usually make great sidekicks (they mooove too slowly), so we decided to leave them out of this adventure.

Raw (mostly!)

Super powers require super fuel – the kind that raw, enzyme-rich food provides. We aim to keep our products as raw as possible, so you can keep on fighting when everyone else calls it a day.

Vegan (mostly!)

We want our heroes to maximise their plant-based powers. Occasionally, however, we call on our black and yellow buzzing friends to help with an extra immunity and natural sweetness boost.

Storage instructions

Lairs, hideouts, headquarters… every superhero needs a place where he can feel safe, in control, and comfortable to be himself. Our heroic treats are no different. To ensure that your Whoa!lesome snacks’ superpowers (and flavours) are kept in tip top shape, store them one of their top three hideouts often:

Frost Fortress

For optimum shelf life, your treats can escape to the icy solitude of your home freezer. Can be frozen for up to 6 months.

Cooling Cave

For easy access, store any leftover treats in your fridge for up to a week from the day of purchase.

Belly Base

The best place for any Whoa!lesome treat – straight down throat alley and into your belly!
